Machine Maintenance

Leadwell Mill 1 and 2

Maintenance Point Image Maintenance Point Description Maintenance Procedure/ Oil Type Frequency
Right back corner of machine by chip bin. Vactra #2 or Magna SW 68 Way Oil Check Daily
Check at back right corner of the machine. Castrol - Hysol MB 50 Coolant, use refractometer to make sure concentration of coolant is between 6% and 12%. Do NOT file while machine is running. Use caution when filling tank with the garden hose, this is a good way to spend the day mopping the whole shop floor! Check weekly in Winter, and daily during the summer.
Air Filters are located on the back of the machine by the power switch and on the spindle cooler. To clean these screens use dawn dish soap and the soft brush at the hand washing sink. Gently scrub both sides of the screens and then dry with compressed air. Re-install screens on machines. Daily
Chip Bin, located on the back right corner of the machine. Chips should be dumped into the proper bins through the doors at the back of the shop. Daily as needed. If bin is close to half full at the beginnning of the shift, it should be emptied.
